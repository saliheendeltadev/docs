---
title: "Paths"
icon: road
description: Paths are made up of `collection` name and `id`.
---

`path = <collection>/<id>`

A builder with `id` as `zPMV1taAk2v7z561mZqi` will have `path='Builders/zPMV1taAk2v7z561mZqi'` since builders collection name is `Builders`

Check out the full list of collections available [here](/reference/collections)
